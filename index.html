About

I'm a developer and founder based in London, studying Computer Science at the University of Manchester while on placement at Airbus Defence & Space.

I build things that ship. Co-founder of Torch0 — a structured market graph for deep-tech that resolves companies, people, and claims into a queryable, cited knowledge base. Also built CodeWave because I got tired of AI development workflows not working properly. Co-founded Digested AI, an AI content curation platform with paying subscribers. Previously built ML APIs processing 10,000+ daily requests, wrote embedded C firmware for a Formula Student electric racecar, and shipped projects across the full stack.

I think a lot about ontology — how systems model what they operate on, and why getting the representation right matters before the automation.

I care about tools that are decoupled, model-agnostic, and open. I believe in shipping fast — momentum fixes most things.
